Expertise: We are a multidisciplinary team of cosmologists, data scientists and engineers

Methods: We use deep learning, Bayesian inference and high performance computing for cosmological data analysis

Code development: We pledge to release our tools and software in open source for the community

Collaborations: We partner with researchers all over the globe, especially among the Rubin/LSST DESC and Euclid communities
